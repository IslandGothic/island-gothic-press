About

Welcome to Island Gothic Press.

Island Gothic is an independent printing press publishing limited-run zines, short fiction, and novels rooted in adventure, psychological fracture, isolation, and modern desolation.

Founded by Joss Berrett, the press serves as both an imprint and a creative laboratory exploring pulp adventure, the supernatural and island decay, inspired by years of living and travelling throughout Southeast Asia.
